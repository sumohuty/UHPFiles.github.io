9-9-2021 Julien Gauthier frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning left wing Julien Gauthier feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Julien Gauthier was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. The 23 year old left wing has not yet played for the Tampa Bay Lightning this season.
