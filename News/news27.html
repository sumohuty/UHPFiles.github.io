24-6-2022 Julien Gauthier frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning left wing Julien Gauthier feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Julien Gauthier was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 84 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 7 assists along with 32 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing is expecting a good season for the team.
