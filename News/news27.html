28-6-2018 Jonathan Drouin frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning left wing Jonathan Drouin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jonathan Drouin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 173 NHL games during his career, notching 30 goals and adding 80 assists along with 98 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
