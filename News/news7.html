20-6-2021 Anthony Cirelli frustrated
Chicago Blackhawks left wing Anthony Cirelli feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Anthony Cirelli was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 342 NHL games during his career, notching 61 goals and adding 93 assists along with 193 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 82 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 17+21=38 with 40 PIM.
