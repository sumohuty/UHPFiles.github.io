28-6-2020 Joni Tuulola frustrated

A native of Finland, Joni Tuulola was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 6th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
