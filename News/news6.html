A native of Canada, Alex Petrovic was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 2nd round of the 2010 entry draft. A native of Canada, Travis Sanheim was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 156 NHL games during his career, notching 9 goals and adding 40 assists along with 82 penalty minutes.
