30-8-2017 Michael Frolik frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes right wing Michael Frolik feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Czech, Michael Frolik was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 576 NHL games during his career, notching 110 goals and adding 157 assists along with 179 penalty minutes. The 29 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
