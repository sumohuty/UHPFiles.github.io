27-6-2022 Aleksi Saarela frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes center Aleksi Saarela feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Aleksi Saarela was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 25 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.
