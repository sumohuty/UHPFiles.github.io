25-9-2018 Michael McCarron frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes right wing Michael McCarron feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Michael McCarron was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 34 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 2 assists along with 37 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the Carolina Hurricanes this season.
