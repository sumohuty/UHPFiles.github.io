28-6-2021 Madison Bowey frustrated
Vancouver Canucks defenseman Madison Bowey feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Madison Bowey was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 169 NHL games during his career, notching 2 goals and adding 17 assists along with 59 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
