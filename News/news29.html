28-6-2018 Artem Anisimov frustrated
Vancouver Canucks center Artem Anisimov feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Artem Anisimov was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 2nd round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 572 NHL games during his career, notching 118 goals and adding 141 assists along with 160 penalty minutes. The 30 year old center is expecting a good season for the team.
