29-6-2018 Reid Boucher frustrated
Dallas Stars left wing Reid Boucher feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Reid Boucher was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 4th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 156 NHL games during his career, notching 29 goals and adding 40 assists along with 60 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing is expecting a good season for the team.
