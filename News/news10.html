13-9-2020 Markus Ruusu frustrated
Dallas Stars goaltender Markus Ruusu feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Markus Ruusu was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 6th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old goaltender has not yet played for the Dallas Stars this season.

8-9-2020 Petr Mrazek frustrated
Dallas Stars goaltender Petr Mrazek feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a core player.

A native of Czech, Petr Mrazek was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 5th round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 316 NHL games during his career, booking 17 career shutouts and allowing 840 goals. The 28 year old goaltender has not yet played for the Dallas Stars this season.
