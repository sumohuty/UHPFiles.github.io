25-9-2018 Player of the week: Tyler Johnson (C)
NHL has awarded Tyler Johnson (C) of Dallas Stars as Player Of The Week He registered 5 goals and 2 assists for 7 points in 3 games last week. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of USA, Tyler Johnson, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 330 NHL games during his career, notching 101 goals and adding 145 assists along with 102 penalty minutes. The 28 year old center has played 7 games with the Dallas Stars this season, totalling 6+4=10 with 0 PIM.
