Boston Bruins defenseman Julius Honka feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Julius Honka was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 125 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 32 assists along with 85 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
