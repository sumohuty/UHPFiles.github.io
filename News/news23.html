29-8-2021 Joshua Morrissey frustrated
Arizona Coyotes defenseman Joshua Morrissey feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Joshua Morrissey was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 333 NHL games during his career, notching 38 goals and adding 124 assists along with 138 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
