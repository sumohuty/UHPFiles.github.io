17-9-2020 Keegan Kolesar frustrated
Minnesota Wild right wing Keegan Kolesar feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Keegan Kolesar was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the Minnesota Wild this season.
