27-6-2018 Jonas Brodin frustrated
Minnesota Wild defenseman Jonas Brodin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Jonas Brodin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 1993 entry draft. He has appeared in 96 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 17 assists along with 42 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman is looking forward for the start of the season.
