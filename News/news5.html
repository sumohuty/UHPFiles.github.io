29-6-2021 Sam Bennett frustrated
Calgary Flames center Sam Bennett feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Sam Bennett was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 411 NHL games during his career, notching 131 goals and adding 186 assists along with 166 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.
