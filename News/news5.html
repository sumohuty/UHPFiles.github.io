29-6-2018 Matt DeBlouw frustrated
Calgary Flames center Matt DeBlouw feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Matt DeBlouw was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 7th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
