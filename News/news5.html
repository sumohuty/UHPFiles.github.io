28-6-2022 Zach Nastasiuk frustrated
Calgary Flames right wing Zach Nastasiuk feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Zach Nastasiuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 37 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 2 assists along with 12 penalty minutes. The 27 year old right wing is waiting for a good start this season.
24-6-2022 Sergei Vinogradov frustrated
Calgary Flames goaltender Sergei Vinogradov feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Sergei Vinogradov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 5th round of the 2017 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old goaltender is getting ready for the upcoming season.
