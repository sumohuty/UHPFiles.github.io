22-6-2018 Brendan Leipsic frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs center Brendan Leipsic feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Brendan Leipsic was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 3rd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 6 NHL games during his career, notching 1 goals and adding 2 assists along with 2 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
