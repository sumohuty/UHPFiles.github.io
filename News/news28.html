28-6-2020 Martins Dzierkals frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs left wing Martins Dzierkals feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Latvia, Martins Dzierkals was originally drafted to the NHL by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old left wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.

22-6-2020 Zach Bogosian frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs defenseman Zach Bogosian feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a core player.

A native of USA, Zach Bogosian was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 734 NHL games during his career, notching 66 goals and adding 200 assists along with 530 penalty minutes. The 29 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
