20-6-2018 Jonathan Ericsson frustrated
Detroit Red Wings defenseman Jonathan Ericsson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Jonathan Ericsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 9th round of the 2002 entry draft. He has appeared in 500 NHL games during his career, notching 23 goals and adding 82 assists along with 388 penalty minutes. The 34 year old defenseman has played 22 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 3+4=7 with 2 PIM.
