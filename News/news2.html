29-6-2022 Joel Armia frustrated
Winnipeg Jets right wing Joel Armia feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Finland, Joel Armia was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 466 NHL games during his career, notching 159 goals and adding 159 assists along with 295 penalty minutes. The 29 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
