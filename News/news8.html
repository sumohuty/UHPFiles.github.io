21-6-2017 Cody Goloubef frustrated
Colorado Avalanche defenseman Cody Goloubef feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Cody Goloubef was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 2nd round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 96 NHL games during his career, notching 2 goals and adding 16 assists along with 41 penalty minutes. The 27 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
