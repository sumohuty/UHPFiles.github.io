30-8-2020 Colorado Avalanche trade
Colorado Avalanche traded Jimmy Hayes and COL's round 3 pick in 2021 to Boston Bruins for Alec Martinez

A native of USA, Alec Martinez was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 4th round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 587 NHL games during his career, notching 56 goals and adding 145 assists along with 144 penalty minutes. The 33 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.

A native of USA, Jimmy Hayes was originally drafted to the NHL by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 2nd round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 477 NHL games during his career, notching 77 goals and adding 86 assists along with 182 penalty minutes. The 30 year old left wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
