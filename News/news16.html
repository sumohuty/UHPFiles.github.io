20-6-2018 Henrik Zetterberg frustrated
Montreal Canadiens left wing Henrik Zetterberg feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Henrik Zetterberg was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 7th round of the 1999 entry draft. He has appeared in 1000 NHL games during his career, notching 323 goals and adding 544 assists along with 415 penalty minutes. The 37 year old left wing has played 82 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 14+17=31 with 50 PIM.
