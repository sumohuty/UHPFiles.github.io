29-6-2018 Thomas Vanek frustrated
Florida Panthers right wing Thomas Vanek feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Austria, Thomas Vanek was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 1st round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 899 NHL games during his career, notching 334 goals and adding 347 assists along with 513 penalty minutes. The 34 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
