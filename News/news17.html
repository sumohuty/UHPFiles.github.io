22-6-2021 Matthew Murray frustrated
New York Islanders goaltender Matthew Murray feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Matthew Murray was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 4th round of the 2016 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old goaltender is getting ready for the upcoming season.
