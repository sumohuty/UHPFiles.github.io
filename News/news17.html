2-9-2022 Vili Saarijarvi frustrated
New York Islanders defenseman Vili Saarijarvi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Vili Saarijarvi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 79 NHL games during his career, notching 4 goals and adding 22 assists along with 30 penalty minutes. The 25 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
