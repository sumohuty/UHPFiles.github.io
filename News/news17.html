23-6-2020 Nathan Noel frustrated
New York Islanders center Nathan Noel feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Nathan Noel was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 3rd round of the 2018 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old center is expecting a good season for the team.
