20-6-2018 Jay Bouwmeester frustrated
St. Louis Blues defenseman Jay Bouwmeester feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jay Bouwmeester was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2002 entry draft. He has appeared in 1072 NHL games during his career, notching 85 goals and adding 305 assists along with 545 penalty minutes. The 34 year old defenseman has played 82 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 4+10=14 with 14 PIM.
