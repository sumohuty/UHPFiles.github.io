20-6-2018 Joonas Lyytinen frustrated
Nashville Predators defenseman Joonas Lyytinen feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Joonas Lyytinen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 5th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Nashville Predators this season.
