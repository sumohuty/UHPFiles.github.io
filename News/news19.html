27-6-2018 Viktor Arvidsson frustrated
Nashville Predators right wing Viktor Arvidsson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Sweden, Viktor Arvidsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 144 NHL games during his career, notching 34 goals and adding 44 assists along with 89 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
