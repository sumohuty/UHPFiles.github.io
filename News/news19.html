29-8-2020 Dylan Strome frustrated
Nashville Predators center Dylan Strome feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Dylan Strome was originally drafted to the NHL by the Arizona Coyotes in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 233 NHL games during his career, notching 68 goals and adding 105 assists along with 4 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center is getting ready for the upcoming season.
