24-6-2022 Owen Tippett frustrated
Edmonton Oilers right wing Owen Tippett feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Owen Tippett was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 164 NHL games during his career, notching 11 goals and adding 12 assists along with 28 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
