29-8-2020 Edmonton Oilers trade
Edmonton Oilers traded Zach Werenski and Bo Horvat to Washington Capitals for Bryan Rust and Bode Wilde

A native of USA, Bryan Rust was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 3rd round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 342 NHL games during his career, notching 54 goals and adding 50 assists along with 160 penalty minutes. The 28 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.

A native of USA, Bode Wilde was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2018 entry draft. He has appeared in 114 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 37 assists along with 37 penalty minutes. The 20 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.

A native of USA, Zach Werenski was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 185 NHL games during his career, notching 23 goals and adding 73 assists along with 89 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.

A native of Canada, Bo Horvat was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 388 NHL games during his career, notching 78 goals and adding 143 assists along with 100 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
