6-9-2020 Mathew Barzal frustrated
Detroit Red Wings right wing Mathew Barzal feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Mathew Barzal was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 259 NHL games during his career, notching 68 goals and adding 97 assists along with 184 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the Detroit Red Wings this season.
