26-6-2022 Conor Garland frustrated
New Jersey Devils left wing Conor Garland feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Conor Garland was originally drafted to the NHL by the Arizona Coyotes in the 5th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 216 NHL games during his career, notching 25 goals and adding 33 assists along with 158 penalty minutes. The 26 year old left wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
