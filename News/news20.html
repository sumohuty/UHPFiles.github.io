20-6-2018 Seth Griffith frustrated
New Jersey Devils right wing Seth Griffith feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Seth Griffith was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 34 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 5 assists along with 10 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has not yet played for the New Jersey Devils this season. He has played 69 games with the Albany Devils this season, totalling 13+21=34 with 2 PIM.
