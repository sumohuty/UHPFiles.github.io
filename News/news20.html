29-8-2020 New Jersey Devils trade
New Jersey Devils traded Luke Kunin and NJ's round 1 pick in 2021 to Vancouver Canucks for David Krejci

A native of Czech, David Krejci was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 879 NHL games during his career, notching 230 goals and adding 430 assists along with 349 penalty minutes. The 34 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.

A native of USA, Luke Kunin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 35 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 5 assists along with 20 penalty minutes. The 22 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
