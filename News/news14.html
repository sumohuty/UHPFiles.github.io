29-6-2018 Jeff Zatkoff frustrated
Los Angeles Kings goaltender Jeff Zatkoff feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Jeff Zatkoff was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 3rd round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 35 NHL games during his career, booking 1 career shutouts and allowing 86 goals. The 31 year old goaltender is expecting a good season for the team.
