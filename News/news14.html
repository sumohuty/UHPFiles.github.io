Los Angeles Kings left wing Jakub Vrana feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Czech, Jakub Vrana was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft.
