30-8-2020 Jakub Zboril frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers defenseman Jakub Zboril feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Czech, Jakub Zboril was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 178 NHL games during his career, notching 16 goals and adding 54 assists along with 134 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
