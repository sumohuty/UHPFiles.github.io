27-6-2021 Taylor Raddysh frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers right wing Taylor Raddysh feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Taylor Raddysh was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 2nd round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 164 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 19 assists along with 112 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing is waiting for a good start this season.
