20-6-2021 Tomas Jurco frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets right wing Tomas Jurco feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Slovakia, Tomas Jurco was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 283 NHL games during his career, notching 26 goals and adding 49 assists along with 84 penalty minutes. The 28 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Columbus Blue Jackets this season, totalling 4+9=13 with 20 PIM.
