20-6-2018 Alexander Chmelevski looking good
Reports from our scouting staff indicate that center Alexander Chmelevski might be develope into a greater talent than expected. His strong work ethic has given him more self confidence.

A native of USA, Alexander Chmelevski was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 19 year old center has not yet played for the Columbus Blue Jackets this season. He has played 74 games with the Springfield Falcons this season, totalling 10+17=27 with 42 PIM.
