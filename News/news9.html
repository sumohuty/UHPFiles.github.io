28-6-2018 Nick Moutrey frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets right wing Nick Moutrey feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Nick Moutrey was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 4th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing is waiting for a good start this season.
