A native of Canada, Brendan Gallagher was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 5th round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 534 NHL games during his career, notching 170 goals and adding 225 assists along with 449 penalty minutes.
