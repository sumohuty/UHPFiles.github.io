26-6-2022 Jordan Kyrou frustrated
Buffalo Sabres right wing Jordan Kyrou feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jordan Kyrou was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 2nd round of the 2016 entry draft.
