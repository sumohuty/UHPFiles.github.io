25-6-2021 Anthony Mantha frustrated
New York Rangers right wing Anthony Mantha feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Anthony Mantha was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 328 NHL games during his career, notching 116 goals and adding 125 assists along with 189 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
