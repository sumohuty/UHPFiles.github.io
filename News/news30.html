28-6-2018 Nicklas Grossmann frustrated
Washington Capitals defenseman Nicklas Grossmann feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Nicklas Grossmann was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 2nd round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 627 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 76 assists along with 324 penalty minutes. The 33 year old defenseman is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
26-6-2018 Joseph Labate frustrated
Washington Capitals left wing Joseph Labate feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Joseph Labate was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 4th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 24 NHL games during his career, notching 1 goals and adding 2 assists along with 6 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
