28-6-2018 Chris Tierney frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins center Chris Tierney feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Chris Tierney was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 142 NHL games during his career, notching 14 goals and adding 32 assists along with 40 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
