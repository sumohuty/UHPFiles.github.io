21-6-2018 Derek Forbort frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins defenseman Derek Forbort feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Derek Forbort was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 67 NHL games during his career, notching 2 goals and adding 7 assists along with 23 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
