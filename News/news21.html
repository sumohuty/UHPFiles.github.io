28-6-2018 Luke Glendening frustrated
Ottawa Senators center Luke Glendening feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Luke Glendening, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 277 NHL games during his career, notching 29 goals and adding 35 assists along with 128 penalty minutes. The 29 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.
