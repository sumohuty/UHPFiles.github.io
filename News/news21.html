26-6-2022 Avery Peterson frustrated
Ottawa Senators center Avery Peterson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Avery Peterson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 6th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 68 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 7 assists along with 18 penalty minutes. The 27 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.
