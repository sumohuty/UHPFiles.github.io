Ottawa Senators right wing Joel Armia feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Joel Armia was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 120 NHL games during his career, notching 20 goals and adding 24 assists along with 54 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
