29-6-2020 Garrett Metcalf frustrated
Ottawa Senators goaltender Garrett Metcalf feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Garrett Metcalf was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 6th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old goaltender is waiting for a good start this season.
