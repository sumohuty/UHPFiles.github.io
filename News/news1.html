5-11-2020 Timo Meier frustrated
Anaheim Ducks right wing Timo Meier feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Switzerland, Timo Meier was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 177 NHL games during his career, notching 46 goals and adding 54 assists along with 152 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing has played 16 games with the Anaheim Ducks this season, totalling 3+7=10 with 25 PIM.
