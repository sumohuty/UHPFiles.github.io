23-6-2021 Tanner Kaspick frustrated
Anaheim Ducks center Tanner Kaspick feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Tanner Kaspick was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 3rd round of the 2017 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
