20-6-2018 Ryan Getzlaf frustrated
Anaheim Ducks center Ryan Getzlaf feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Ryan Getzlaf was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 869 NHL games during his career, notching 255 goals and adding 560 assists along with 715 penalty minutes. The 33 year old center has played 82 games with the Anaheim Ducks this season, totalling 34+40=74 with 34 PIM.
