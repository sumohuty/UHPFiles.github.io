28-6-2020 Brett Lernout frustrated
Anaheim Ducks defenseman Brett Lernout feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Brett Lernout was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 3rd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 106 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 12 assists along with 28 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.

21-6-2020 Alex Galchenyuk frustrated
Anaheim Ducks center Alex Galchenyuk feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Alex Galchenyuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft.
