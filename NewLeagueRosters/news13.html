23-6-2018 Derek MacKenzie frustrated

A native of Canada, Derek MacKenzie was originally drafted to the NHL by the Atlanta Thrashers in the 5th round of the 1999 entry draft. He has appeared in 479 NHL games during his career, notching 50 goals and adding 64 assists along with 272 penalty minutes. The 37 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.
