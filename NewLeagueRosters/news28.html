24-6-2018 Toronto Maple Leafs trade
Toronto Maple Leafs traded Matt Martin to Edmonton Oilers for EDM's round 5 pick in 2019

A native of Canada, Matt Martin was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 5th round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 492 NHL games during his career, notching 44 goals and adding 50 assists along with 696 penalty minutes. The 29 year old left wing is expecting a good season for the team.

Toronto Maple Leafs traded William Nylander to Detroit Red Wings for Jacob Trouba

A native of USA, Jacob Trouba was originally drafted to the NHL by the Atlanta Thrashers in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 293 NHL games during his career, notching 31 goals and adding 76 assists along with 203 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
