30-8-2018 New York Rangers trade
New York Rangers traded NYR's round 3 pick in 2019 to Phoenix Coyotes for Ryan Reaves

A native of Canada, Ryan Reaves was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 5th round of the 2005 entry draft. He has appeared in 421 NHL games during his career, notching 25 goals and adding 28 assists along with 639 penalty minutes. The 31 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
30-8-2018 New York Rangers trade
New York Rangers traded Kevin Shattenkirk to Detroit Red Wings for DET's round 1 pick in 2019

A native of USA, Kevin Shattenkirk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 492 NHL games during his career, notching 57 goals and adding 219 assists along with 309 penalty minutes. The 29 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
30-8-2018 New York Rangers trade
New York Rangers traded Anthony DeAngelo to Minnesota Wild for Kristian Vesalainen

A native of USA, Anthony DeAngelo was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 24 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 10 assists along with 16 penalty minutes. The 22 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
