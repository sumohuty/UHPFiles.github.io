26-6-2018 Andrew Hammond frustrated
Ottawa Senators goaltender Andrew Hammond feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Andrew Hammond, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 71 NHL games during his career, booking 6 career shutouts and allowing 158 goals. The 30 year old goaltender is waiting for a good start this season.
25-6-2018 Nick Paul frustrated
Ottawa Senators left wing Nick Paul feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Nick Paul was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 4th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 51 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 4 assists along with 8 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing is expecting a good season for the team.
