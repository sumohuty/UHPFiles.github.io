30-8-2018 Edmonton Oilers trade
Edmonton Oilers traded Caleb Jones and Ziyat Paigin to Montreal Canadiens for Nicolas Deslauriers and MTL's round 3 pick in 2019

A native of Canada, Nicolas Deslauriers was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 3rd round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 251 NHL games during his career, notching 25 goals and adding 31 assists along with 182 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing is waiting for a good start this season.

A native of USA, Caleb Jones was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 4th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 21 year old defenseman is looking forward for the start of the season.

A native of Russia, Ziyat Paigin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 7th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is looking forward for the start of the season.
