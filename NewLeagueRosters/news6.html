29-6-2018 Morgan Klimchuk frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes left wing Morgan Klimchuk feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Morgan Klimchuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old left wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
