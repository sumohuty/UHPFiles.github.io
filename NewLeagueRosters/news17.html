27-6-2018 Marek Mazanec frustrated
New York Islanders goaltender Marek Mazanec feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Czech, Marek Mazanec was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 6th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 27 NHL games during his career, booking 2 career shutouts and allowing 68 goals. The 26 year old goaltender is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
25-6-2018 Casey Cizikas frustrated
New York Islanders center Casey Cizikas feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Casey Cizikas was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 4th round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 372 NHL games during his career, notching 39 goals and adding 72 assists along with 159 penalty minutes. The 27 year old center is looking forward for the start of the season.
25-6-2018 Cal Clutterbuck frustrated
New York Islanders right wing Cal Clutterbuck feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Cal Clutterbuck was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 3rd round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 647 NHL games during his career, notching 102 goals and adding 80 assists along with 492 penalty minutes. The 30 year old right wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
