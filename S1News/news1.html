25-4-2017 NHL nominates players for NHL Awards
The NHL has released the annual list of award nominees. For each category in which the winner is voted, there are three nominees. The annual NHL Awards Ceremony is held right after the Stanley Cup Finals.

Here are the candidates for season 2016-17:

Jack Adams Award
1. Barry Trotz, Washington Capitals
2. Todd Richards, Columbus Blue Jackets
3. Michel Therrien, Montreal Canadiens
Annual award given to the NHL coach adjudged to have contributed the most to his team's success

Calder Memorial Trophy
1. Dylan Larkin, Tampa Bay Lightning
2. Auston Matthews, Toronto Maple Leafs
3. Connor McDavid, Edmonton Oilers
Annual award given to the player selected as the most proficient in his first year of competition in the National Hockey League

Hart Memorial Trophy
1. Rick Nash, St. Louis Blues
2. Jaden Schwartz, St. Louis Blues
3. Vladimir Tarasenko, St. Louis Blues
Annual award given to the player judged to be the most valuable to his team

Lady Byng Memorial Trophy
1. Tyler Seguin, Dallas Stars
2. Patrick Kane, Chicago Blackhawks
3. Jaden Schwartz, St. Louis Blues
Annual award given to the player adjudged to have exhibited the best type of sportsmanship and gentlemanly conduct combined with a high standard of playing ability

James Norris Memorial Trophy
1. P.K. Subban, Montreal Canadiens
2. Duncan Keith, Chicago Blackhawks
3. Keith Yandle, Florida Panthers
Annual award given to the defense player who demonstrates throughout the season the greatest all-round ability in the position

Frank J. Selke Trophy
1. David Backes, Chicago Blackhawks
2. Cal Clutterbuck, New York Islanders
3. Chris Kreider, New York Rangers
Annual award given to the forward who best excels in the defensive aspects of the game
