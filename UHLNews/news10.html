3-9-2021 Dallas Stars trade
Dallas Stars traded Hampus Lindholm, Bo Horvat and DAL's round 5 pick in 2022 to Arizona Coyotes for ARI's round 5 pick in 2022

A native of Sweden, Hampus Lindholm was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 570 NHL games during his career, notching 68 goals and adding 242 assists along with 315 penalty minutes. The 27 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.

A native of Canada, Bo Horvat was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 410 NHL games during his career, notching 144 goals and adding 201 assists along with 125 penalty minutes. The 26 year old left wing is expecting a good season for the team.
