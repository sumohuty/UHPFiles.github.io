11-2-2018 NHL All-Star Skill Competitions
The annual All-Star event kicked off today with the skill competitions. Team North America and Team World competed in six different races and the fans got to see their stars shine.

Team World won the puck control relay with a team of Evgeni Malkin, Ilya Kovalchuk and Vladimir Tarasenko.

Evgeni Malkin of Team World won the fastest skater competition, beating Taylor Hall in the final.

Alex Ovechkin of Team World won the hardest shot competition with a result of 93 MPH, beating Ryan Getzlaf in the final.

Patrick Kane of Team North America won the accuracy shooting competition, beating Erik Karlsson in the final.

Carey Price of Team North America won the goalie competition (pass&score), beating Henrik Lundqvist in the final.

Team World won the breakaway relay with a team of Evgeni Malkin, Vladimir Tarasenko and Ilya Kovalchuk.

The yearly clash between Team North America and Team World will take place tomorrow.
