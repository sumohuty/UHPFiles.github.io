A native of USA, Stefan Matteau was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 24 NHL games during his career, notching 2 goals and adding 2 assists along with 10 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing is waiting for a good start this season.
